Mi Goreng Noodles

Quick and easy noodles!

Now you would hope people wouldn't need to find a recipe for making instant noodles - it's literally on the package already - but for those of you who may be a little... ...I'm here to help!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Package of IndoMie Mi Goreng Noodles
  • Â½ Tbsp. mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Put dry noodles in water for three minutes.
  3. Add all contents of packets from the bag to a bowl - and mayonnaise if using.
  4. Mix packet contents in bowl.
  5. Drain noodles.
  6. Add noodles to bowl and mix until sauce evenly dispersed.
  7. Eat... enjoy! Do not stray further than one or two minutes from a toilet for approximately 12 hours after consumption.
