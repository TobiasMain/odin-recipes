Ingredients:

  • Brown Play Dough
  • Red Play Dough
  • White Play Dough
  • Any other colour Play Dough for the topping you so desire

Steps:

  1. Flatten dough (brown Play Dough) into large circle.
  2. Flatten sauce (Red Play Dough) into slightly smaller circle and place on the dough.
  3. Flatten cheese (white Play Dough) into a yet slightly smaller circle and play on the sauce.
  4. Create whatever other topping you would like out of the other colours of Play Dough and place on top of cheese.
  5. Enjoy! Probably not good to consume in large quantities... but hey never hurts to try... right?
